Personal Budget

A personal-budget management app

Expenditure at a glance

You get all your expenditures at a place with how much you are spending at a place so that you can view and understand where the money are going

Alerts

What if your expenditure crossed your budget? You will get an alert. The goal is to never go over the budget.

Analysis

Get insights on what you are spending and how much you are spending. Data often is displayed in the form of several graphs to demonstrate data accurately. It is very helpful making them easier to understanding instead of viewing the texts

Free

All the features are free. Product is ours, data is yours. Why should you pay when it is an open source application.

List

Comes with a list feature with which you can enter the list of needed items so that you will buy those and do not go for other unnecessary things while shopping.

Alerts

What if your clothing budget ended? You will get an alert. The goal is to never go over the budget.

Results

People who stick to a financial plan, budgeting every expense, get out of debt faster! Also, they to live happier lives... since they expend without guilt or fear... because they know it is all good and accounted for.
